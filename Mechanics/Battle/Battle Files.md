The game's battle mechanics are stored redundantly in multiple files:
## BATTLE.EMI and BATTLE2.EMI
`/BIN/BATTLE/BATTLE.EMI` and `/BIN/BATTLE/BATTLE2.EMI` are the files used for battle mechanics outside of boss battles pre- and post-timeskip respectively.
